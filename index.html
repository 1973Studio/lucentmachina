About

Lucent Machina is a commissioning platform for illuminated studies of engineered machines. We create restrained, light-driven visualisations that reveal internal structure, flow, and intent — produced as permanent physical objects designed to live in space rather than on a screen.

The work sits between engineering documentation and contemporary object design: precise, factual, and quietly permanent by nature.

Commissions

Response time: typically within 1–2 business days.
Commissions are produced as bespoke, acrylic-mounted display objects with an optional technical plate.
